Target Audience

My target audience will be people between 25 - 80 years old who like to spend their time traveling to cities, people who like to learn about the culture and history of each place they visit. I will focus on people from the middle and upper social class since they are the ones who travel the most. Also this website, being in English, will geographically appeal to English-speaking people. The people who visit the site will be people with particular interests to know Lima, Peru, and also to know what to eat, what to visit, and where to go to have an unforgettable trip.

Personas

Jose Brox

Jose is a 30-year-old entrepreneur who lives with his wife Maria, they both heard that Lima was an imperial city and that there are catacombs with thousands of bones of people who lived there; Jose decides to investigate more to find out what other places they can visit there.

Jade Blink

Jade is 40 years old, she is a hard-working single mother and she is looking for a place to travel with her 2 children. She wants her children to have a lot of fun, but she doesn't want the trip to be just fun, she wants it to be learning. She feels that her children would like to learn more about the Inca culture.
