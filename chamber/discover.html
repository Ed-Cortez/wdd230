Catacombs of Lima

The catacombs or crypts of Lima are considered the largest in the entire continent and also the best preserved. Its vast extension is only surpassed by the famous Catacombs of Paris.

Main Catredal of Lima

It has fifteen chapels inside, one of which houses the remains of the founder of Lima, Francisco Pizarro. It also houses an important museum with a valuable collection of religious art. It is considered to be the most important church in Peru and is one of the most outstanding in the capital.

Lighthouse of Lima

The Lighthouse of Lima, also known as the Faro de la Marina, is a historic lighthouse located in Lima, Peru. It is situated in the Chorrillos district, overlooking the Pacific Ocean. The lighthouse has played an important role in guiding ships along the Peruvian coast for many years.

Magic Water Circuit

The Magic Water Circuit in Parque de la Reserva was verified by the Guinness Book of World Records as the largest complex of fountains in the world in a public park. It consists of 13 water fountains that combine music, colored lights and laser effects for a must visit for families in Lima.
